Net Salary BG — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-30

Net Salary BG does not collect any data. The app performs every calculation entirely on your device. It has no accounts, no login, no analytics, no tracking, and no advertising, and it does not transmit your inputs anywhere.

Data we collect

None. Any values you enter stay on your device (in local app storage) and are never sent to us or to any third party.

Third parties & tracking

The app contains no third-party SDKs, no ad networks, and no tracking technologies. App Tracking Transparency is not requested because nothing is tracked. Our App Store privacy label is therefore Data Not Collected.
